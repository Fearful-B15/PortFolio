Zoopedia

Zoopedia is an educational and informative website I developed as part of a formative assignment. The goal of the project was to build a platform where users can compare different zoos and wildlife parks based on entry prices, available animals, and special features. It also includes detailed pages listing animals per zoo and a side-by-side comparison feature to help families and students plan visits.

Features

  • Animal listings by location
  • Responsive and structured layout for easy navigation
  • Visually categorized zoo features
  • Great for educational and recreational planning
