USN Mobile Fitness App Wireframing project

The USN Mobile Fitness App Wireframing project involved creating a mobile application for USN to create a fitness tracker for their company and customers. The app features a clean, modern design with intuitive navigation and a focus on user experience.

Overview

The main goal of the USN Mobile Fitness App is to provide users with a comprehensive fitness tracking solution that integrates seamlessly with USN's existing systems. The app allows users to monitor their physical activity, set fitness goals, and track their progress over time. Built with modern development practices, the app emphasizes usability, performance, and scalability.

USN Mobile Fitness App Design & Features

  • Fitness tracking capabilities
  • Structured interface for easy navigation and use
  • Product and Company newsletter
  • Diet Planning with Meal Tracking and calories for each meal
